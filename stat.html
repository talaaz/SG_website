Understanding the network

The network contains 4.202 nodes representated by users and 6.846 edges connecting users, In order to analyze the network a weighted graph over the whole network of stackoverflow commuinty is created, The users nodes are colored by the the language they are mostly related to, and edges linking users across langauges are colored in black. One can clearly see that users tend to interact with users of same langauge, though, users 'Python' (blue) and 'Javascript' (red) tend to interact more. Thus, it can be said that 'Python' and 'Javascript' have more in common, which makes sense since the syntaxes are very similar.

Understanding the text

The text derived for this project, is the text from the questions asked, answers given, and what the different users have written about themselves. On average 4 words are used to form a question, 190 words on average to elaborate a question and 61 words on average to answer a question. Though, these numbers come with bias, as stopwords has been removed from the text. Thus, the numbers can be viewed to describe only descriptive words used.
